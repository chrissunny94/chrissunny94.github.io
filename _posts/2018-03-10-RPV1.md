We developed UGV from the scratch. We designed and Developed the working UGV in house with following Specification:

Material MS Steel (powder coated)
Wheel size 8 inch (2.5/4 N8)
Wheel type Pneumatic (Nylon)
Robot mass ~30kgs
Chassis payload 500kgs
Carrying payload (motorized) 50kgs
Clearance 75 mm
Max speed 1.2 m/s

A quick insight into the product functionalities -
 Flexibility with the development platforms (Raspberry Pi, Jetson TX1, Laptops etc)
 Multiple sensors (Proximity, IMU, encoders, temperature, LEDs, Cameras (for slam,
object tracking, and visual functionalities), motor control, PID motion control, WIFI
connectivity, bluetooth, etc)
Optional:
 Variable mechanical specs based on requirement (Payload, inclination etc)
 Variable hardware specs based on requirement (Onboard computer, additional sensors
like kinect / realsense / z stereo camera / sweep lidar)
